### {{page-title}}

The {{pagelink:DataStandardsWales-Organization, text: Organization}} resource contains information about health and care organizations. Examples for Wales include Health Boards and NHS Trusts, Hospitals, Dental practices, GP practices and primary care clusters, but could be any public, private or voluntary sector organization whose activities encompass the funding or provision of health care services.
