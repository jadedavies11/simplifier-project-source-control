### {{page-title}}

The {{pagelink:DataStandardsWales-Organization, text: Organization}} resource contains information about health and care organizations. In Wales this may include Health Boards and NHS Trusts, Hospitals, Dental practices, GP practices and primary care clusters, but could be any public, private or voluntary sector organization whose activities encompass the funding or provision of health care services. To aid Organization recognition and retrieval, the `Organization.type` element carries structured information defining the sector, domain, classification and subclassification.  Each `alias` can also be classified using an `aliasType` extension.
